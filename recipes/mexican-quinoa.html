One-Skillet Mexican Quinoa

Description

This Mexican quinoa dish is one of those one-skillet recipes that's perfect for feeding a large family. And the kids love it! Serve it for Cinco de Mayo or any night of the week.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 medium jalapeño pepper, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 1 (15 ounce) can black beans, rinsed and draine
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can fire-roasted diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup yellow corn
  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoon red pepper flakes, or to taste
  • 1 ½ teaspoons chili powder ½
  • kosher salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 medium avocado - peeled, pitted, and diced
  • 1 medium lime, juiced

Steps

  1. step 1 Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Sauté jalapeño pepper and garlic in the hot oil until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  2. step 2 Stir black beans, tomatoes, corn, quinoa, and chicken broth into the skillet. Season with pepper flakes, chili powder, cumin, salt, and black pepper; bring to a boil.
  3. step 3 Stir black beans, tomatoes, corn, quinoa, and chicken broth into the skillet. Season with pepper flakes, chili powder, cumin, salt, and black pepper; bring to a boil.
  4. step 4 Add avocado, lime juice, and cilantro; stir until combined.
